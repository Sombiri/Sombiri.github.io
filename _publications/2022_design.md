excerpt: "Various encoding algorithms are generally required to store digital information in DNA and to maintain data integrity. Since DNA is the information carrier, its performance under different processing and storage conditions significantly impacts the capabilities of the data storage system. Therefore, the design of a DNA storage system must meet specific design considerations to be less error-prone, robust and reliable."
